Daily Calibration

Step 1: Prepare Scale
  • Place portafilter or grinder cup on scale
  • Press TARE to zero the scale
  • Ensure grinder hopper has beans
  • Bean storage: Coffee bags (regular & decaf) are under the counter below the grinder
Step 2: Test Shot
  • Grind for 2 seconds
  • Check weight on scale
  • Target: 18-19 grams
Step 3: Adjust if Needed
  • Less than 18g? Grind slightly longer OR adjust grind time dial
  • More than 19g? Grind slightly shorter OR adjust grind time dial
  • Repeat test shot until consistently hitting 18-19g

Throughout the Day

Monitor Consistency
  • Check dose weight every hour
  • Beans settle in hopper ‚Üí may need minor adjustment
  • If espresso shots timing changes, recheck grind

Troubleshooting

  • Shots pulling too fast (under 20 sec)? Grind finer
  • Shots pulling too slow (over 30 sec)? Grind coarser
  • Inconsistent weights? Clean burrs, check hopper level
  • Grinder jamming? Stop, clean burrs, check for foreign objects

  • Closing: Turn off grinder, brush clean, empty grounds bin
