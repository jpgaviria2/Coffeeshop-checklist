Commercial brewer setup and operation

Step 1: Prep the Brewer
  • Ensure brewer is plugged in and warmed up (20 minutes)
  • Remove coffee basket and drip container
  • Top up water reservoir with 200-300ml fresh water
  • Run flush cycle (no coffee) to clean lines
Step 2: Measure Coffee
  • Amount: 70 grams of coffee beans
  • Water: 1.9 liters
  • Batches needed: 2 batches in the morning
  • Use scale to weigh beans accurately
Step 3: Grind & Brew
  • Grind 70g of beans (medium-coarse grind)
  • Place filter in basket
  • Add ground coffee to filter basket
  • Level the grounds (don't compact)
  • Insert basket into brewer
  • Start brew cycle
Step 4: Quality Check
  • Brew time should be approximately 4-5 minutes
  • Check color: rich, dark brown (not pale)
  • Taste test for balance
  • Ensure full pot brewed (1.9L)
Step 5: Repeat for Second Batch
  • Discard used grounds and filter
  • Rinse basket
  • Repeat steps 2-4 for second batch
  • Ensure both batches ready before opening

Daily Maintenance

  • Morning: Flush brewer before first batch
  • Throughout day: Empty drip container as needed
  • Closing: Clean basket, unplug brewer
