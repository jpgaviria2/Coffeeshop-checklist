## Structural Gaps

### ⚠️ No Health Binder Digital Integration
- Currently: "record in Health Binder" (paper binder, physical)  
- **Opportunity**: Use the pass/fail photo evidence to create a **digital food safety log** — photo of thermometer = proof of temp check

### ⚠️ No Shift Handover Notes
- There's no structured place for closers to leave notes for openers  
- **Proposed**: Add a mandatory "Shift Notes" text field to closing submission — surfaces in the opener's view

### ⚠️ No Mid-Shift Checklist
- Opening and closing are covered; nothing for mid-shift tasks  
- **Gap**: Sanitizer spray bottle refresh (every 2 hours per FOODSAFE), mid-day restocks, compost checks

### ⚠️ FOODSAFE Compliance Items Underdocumented
- No explicit time/temp log format  
- No documented allergen procedures  
- No documented handwashing reminders per FOODSAFE BC
