Plate Organization Requirements

  • 🍽️ White plates: Stacked by size, largest at bottom
  • ⚫ Black plates: Separate stack, organized by size
  • 📏 Consistent stacks: Edges aligned, stable piles
  • 🥣 Bowls: Nested properly, easy to grab
  • 🧼 All clean & dry: No water spots or food residue
  • 📦 Shelf organized: Everything in designated spot
  • ✨ Accessible: Most-used items at front/easy height
💡 Why this matters: Organized plates mean faster service, no hunting for clean dishes during rush, and professional presentation. This organization saves time every single shift!

Closing Checklist for Dishes

  • Wash all dishes before organizing
  • Dry completely (no stacking wet plates)
  • Sort by type and size
  • Stack according to photo standard
  • Ensure stability (won't tip or fall)
  • Place on shelf in designated locations
