üìã Important Notes

Domo Tea Products
  • We use Domo brand loose powder for Matcha, Chai, and London Fog
Cappuccino vs Latte Milk Amounts
  • 12oz: Latte = 300ml milk, Cappuccino = 200ml milk (100ml less)
  • 16oz: Latte = 415ml milk, Cappuccino = 270ml milk (145ml less)
  • Cappuccino has LESS milk but MORE foam. Steam with lots of air to create thick, dry foam.
  • Traditional cappuccino ratio: 1/3 espresso, 1/3 steamed milk, 1/3 foam
Special Size Restrictions
  • Flat White: Only available in 12oz. Use long shot (hold middle button)
  • Cortado: Only available in small size. Equal parts espresso and steamed milk
Milk Options
  • Whole, 2%, oat, almond, soy available for all drinks
Ice Guidelines
  • Fill cup 3/4 full with ice for all iced drinks
üí° Pro Tips
  • Steaming milk: For cappuccinos, introduce more air during the "texture phase" (first 5-7 seconds) to create thick foam
  • Flat white: Aim for velvety microfoam with minimal foam layer - smoother texture than a latte
  • Cortado: This is a small, strong drink - equal espresso and milk creates a balanced, bold flavor
  • Iced drinks: Pour milk slowly over ice to prevent splashing
  • Matcha: Whisk powder with a small amount of liquid first to prevent clumps, then add remaining milk
