Daily Calibration

Step 1: Machine Warmup
  • Turn on La Marzocco espresso machine
  • Wait for pressure gauge to stabilize
  • Flush group heads (run water briefly)
  • Wipe group heads clean
Step 2: Grinder Check
  • Verify grinder is calibrated (18-19g per shot)
  • Grind test dose into portafilter
  • Check grind consistency
  • Bean storage: Regular & decaf beans stored under the counter below the grinder
Step 3: Calibrate Extraction
  • Set tamper to position 11
  • Place cup on scale, TARE to zero
  • Grind dose (18-19g) into portafilter
  • Level grounds, tamp at position 11
  • Wipe rim clean
  • Lock into group head
  • Start extraction immediately
  • Target: 38-40g in 20-30 seconds
Step 4: Adjust if Needed
  • Too fast (under 20 sec)? Grind finer
  • Too slow (over 30 sec)? Grind coarser
  • Wrong output weight? Adjust grind dose
  • Repeat until consistently hitting target

Milk Steaming

Steaming Technique
  • Fill pitcher to bottom of spout (for 12oz drink)
  • Purge steam wand before inserting
  • Insert wand tip just below milk surface
  • Texture phase: Incorporate air (5-7 seconds)
    • Keep tip near surface
    • Hear gentle "tss tss" sound
  • Heat phase: Submerge wand deeper
    • Create whirlpool motion
    • Heat to 140-150¬∞F (pitcher hot but touchable)
  • Stop when pitcher too hot to hold comfortably
  • Purge and wipe wand immediately
  • Tap pitcher, swirl to incorporate foam

Drink Assembly

Latte / Cappuccino
  • Pull espresso shot(s) directly into cup
  • Swirl milk pitcher
  • Pour milk from low height initially
  • Latte: More milk, thin foam layer
  • Cappuccino: Equal espresso/milk/foam
  • Optional: latte art
Americano
  • Pull double shot into cup
  • Add hot water to desired strength
  • Leave room for cream if requested

Quality Checks

  • Espresso: Rich crema, tiger striping, smells rich
  • Milk: Glossy, microfoam texture (no large bubbles)
  • Temperature: Hot but drinkable (140-150¬∞F)
  • Taste: Balanced, sweet, not bitter

  • Purge group heads between shots
  • Clean steam wand immediately after each use
  • Pre-heat cups for better temperature
  • Use freshly ground coffee (never let grounds sit)

Troubleshooting

  • Bitter shot? Over-extracted (grind coarser, check temp)
  • Sour shot? Under-extracted (grind finer)
  • Weak shot? Not enough coffee or channeling
  • Big bubbles in milk? Too much air incorporated
  • Flat milk? Not enough aeration or not hot enough

Daily Maintenance

  • Opening: Flush groups, calibrate extraction
  • Throughout day: Purge groups, clean wand after each use
  • Closing: Deep clean groups, install 17g basket, put on standby
