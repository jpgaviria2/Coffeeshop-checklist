Espresso Machine Closing Checklist

  • ✨ Machine polished and shiny (no fingerprints, water spots, or coffee stains)
  • 🧼 Drip trays cleaned and in place
  • ☕ Portafilters clean and positioned properly
  • 🥤 Cups and glasses organized on top (as shown in photo)
  • 🧽 Group heads cleaned, baskets removed and cleaned
  • 💧 Steam wands wiped clean
  • 📏 Work surface around machine clean and organized
  • 🔧 Tampers and tools in their proper positions
💡 Standard: If the station doesn't match this photo, it's not ready for closing. Take the extra time to get it right!
